1. Automatically Enable Low Power Mode

    a. Install Apple's Shortcuts App:

       https://apps.apple.com/app/shortcuts/id915249334

    b. Open Shortcuts and click the Automation button at the 
       bottom

    c. Click 'Create Personal Automation'

    d. Chose 'Battery Level' as the trigger and set the desired 
       'Falls Below' battery level (I use 95% because I see no 
       point in using normal power mode)

    e. Choose 'Run Without Asking' if you are asked whether the 
       automation should ask you before running.

    Source: https://lifehacker.com/you-can-customize-low-power-mode-on-your-iphone-1848030354
