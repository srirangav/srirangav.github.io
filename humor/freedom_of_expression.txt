During a visit to the United States, Premier Brezhnev and President Carter
happened to see a protest. "No Carter, No Reagan!" the protesters shouted. 

"You see," said Carter, "in our country we have freedom of expression, 
something you don't have over in your country."

"Ah, Comrade," replied Brezhnev, "you are wrong! Come over and see!"

So they went to Red Square, and indeed, there was a mob of protesters 
forming, shouting, "Nyet Carter, nyet Reagan!"
