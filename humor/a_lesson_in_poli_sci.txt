A Lesson in Political Science
-----------------------------

Scenario: You have two cows.

Socialism   - The government takes one to give to someone else.
Communism   - The government takes both and gives you the milk.
Fascism     - The government takes both and sells you the milk.
Nazism      - The government takes both and shoots you.
Bureaucracy - The government takes both, shoots one and pours 
              the milk down the drain.
Capitalism  - You sell one and buy a bull.
Anarchy     - You steal neighbour's bull and shoot the government.
