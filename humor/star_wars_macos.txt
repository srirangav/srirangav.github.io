There's a lesson to be found in Star Wars, one that is very important
for Mac OS users. 

You see, Grand Moff Tarkin (Bill Gates) assumes he can cruise around
on his technological monstrosity (the Death Star doing a great
imitation of Windows 95), blowing up planets and wiping out the noble
Rebel Alliance (the Mac OS community). 

In fact, Tarkin views the Rebel's counter attack (the Mac OS) as
insignifigant, and barely makes any effort to stop them from attacking
his station. 

And, of course, in the end, evil is punished, bad guys are killed, and
the good guys get medals (more market share). 

Even though Darth Vader (IBM) escapes, he eventually becomes one of
the good guys, since his son, Luke (the PowerPC processor series) has
sided with the Alliance.
