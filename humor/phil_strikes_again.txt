PHILLIP "EDWARD" NUNEZ STRIKES AGAIN

(Berkeley, CA) 

According to anonymous sources, UC Berkeley super-senior Phillip
Nunez, known by the nickname "Edward", has just completed the
final for Berkeley's Computer Science 162 class entirely in "Red
Violet" crayon. It is as of yet unknown what his precise
motivations were, whether he intends to graduate any time in the
observable future, or what he has been doing in the past 5 years
(since the last time he was observed taking an exam using a red
crayon).
