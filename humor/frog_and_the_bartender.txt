So a frog walks into a bar, slaps down a hundred dollar bill, and says "give
me a beer."  

Bartender hands over a beer and, thinking "what does a frog know about money?"
says "That'll be $100 please."  

The frog gives him the money and starts drinking the beer.  

As he's sitting there the bartender starts to feel a bit guilty about ripping
the frog off, so he makes some smalltalk.  

"We don't get many frogs in here" he says. 

The frog replies, "At prices like these I'm surpised you get anyone."
